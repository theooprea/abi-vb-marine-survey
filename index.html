About Us

We take pride in the work that we do and the services we provide, here are a few words about us

Transfer Capability

We specialise in offering an efficient, safe and cost-effective crew transfer service. Our fleet of vessels are equipped to carry out secure crew transfers day and night, with both safety and comfort in mind. Our highly experienced team can also facilitate the transfer of supplies of goods, up to one tonne.

Crew Safety

As with all our services, safety is our number one priority. Our crew transfer vessels are coded by the Romanian Naval Authority, allowing them to operate up to 20 miles from a safeheaven. All of the safety equipment required is carried on board and serviced to schedule, along with appropiate insurance and safety provisions.

Our Fleet

Our fleet consists of 3 Pilot Boats: Val Petru, Maritim 2 and Maritim 3.

To ensure professional and performant tugs we constantly work in having them in the best shape possible, performing all planned maintenance repairs in Constanta Shipyard, the leading Shipyard of Romania.

Our Work

We provide a number of services, such as dredging assistance, crew change, and transportation of various goods, parts, etc.
